# 📌 Executive Summary

This project demonstrates an **end-to-end Business Analyst workflow** based on operational data from an OLA ride-booking platform.

Using SQL and Power BI, key operational challenges were identified and translated into business requirements, Agile user stories, wireframes, process diagrams, and Jira artifacts.

The project follows the complete Business Analysis lifecycle—from identifying business problems to proposing technology solutions and measuring expected business outcomes.

---

# 🚨 Business Problem

Analysis of 10,000+ ride records identified several operational challenges.

| KPI | Current State |
|------|--------------:|
| Ride Failure Rate | **37%** |
| Customer Cancellations | **22%** |
| Driver Cancellations | **15%** |
| One-Time Users | **53%** |
| Primary Business Challenge | Low customer retention & high ride cancellations |

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Reduce ride cancellations
- Improve fleet utilization
- Increase UPI payment adoption
- Improve customer retention
- Enhance driver responsiveness
- Support data-driven decision making

---

# 💡 Key Business Recommendations

- Introduce cancellation warnings for frequent cancellers.
- Trigger driver inactivity alerts after ride acceptance.
- Optimize vehicle allocation using trip distance.
- Promote UPI with cashback incentives.
- Automate WhatsApp campaigns for first-time riders.
